Krapinjon

Youth organization

The Krapinjon Association is an association for young people in Krapina. For many years, the association has been organizing events to promote culture among young people and how to become an active citizen. The goal of the association is to encourage young people to start creating, which is actually represented by fire in this logo. From the very beginning, fire represented the discovery and innovation that started everything. The slogan "Ignite your fire" in this way encourages young people to find what will motivate them to progress.

2RUN app

Rinning aplication design

2RUN app is a student project in collaboration with a colleague, that presents the idea of a running application that is also a social network. The 2RUN app connects runners in an innovative way and provides them with the opportunity to share achievements and compete with other app users. The design of the application is characterized by a bright red color that represents energy and encourages movement. As part of the group project, my task was to create the logo and user interface of the application.
